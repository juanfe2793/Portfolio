```yaml
  - Career
```

Convert to Docusaurus format (per D-06 converting categories to tags, per D-05 linking author):

4. Remove the trailing `---` and `*Juan Felipe Gomez*` signature at the end of the post (the author is now shown via the `authors` frontmatter field).

5. Keep all other body content exactly as-is (the bullet list of upcoming topics and "Stay tuned" line).
